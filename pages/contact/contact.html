How does the weight calculator work?

The weight calculator determines a person's ideal weight based on their height and gender. The calculator computes the height (in feet & inches) based on the following formulas:
D. R. Miller’s formula (1983)

For women: 53.1 kg + 1.36 kg per inch over 5 feet

For men: 56.2 kg + 1.41 kg per inch over 5 feet

J.D. Robinson’s formula (1983)

For women= 49 kg + 1.7 kg per inch over 5 feet

For men= 52 kg + 1.9 kg per inch over 5 feet

B. J. Devine’s formula (1974)

For women = 45.5 kg + 2.3 kg per inch over 5 feet

For men= 50.0 kg+ 2.3 kg per inch over 5 feet

G. J. Hamwi’s formula (1964)

For women = 45.5 kg + 2.2 kg per inch over 5 feet

For men= 48.0 kg + 2.7 kg per inch over 5 feet

How do I use the weight calculator?

To use the application, simply input your height in feet & inches and your gender. The application will then present your ideal weight according to Miller (1983), Robinson (1983), Devine (1974), and Hamwi (1964).
