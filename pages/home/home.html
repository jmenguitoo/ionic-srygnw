WELCOME TO TRES FITNESS!

In response to the COVID-19 pandemic, Tres Fitness provides health & fitness tips and motivations to people who desire to keep shape at home.

Tres Fitness also provides an Ideal Weight Calculator that uses Professional statistics to determine the weight of a male and female. The formulas utilized in this calculator were from scientists G. J. Hamwi, B. J. Devine, J. D. Robinson, and D. R. Miller.
